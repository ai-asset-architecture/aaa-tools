# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to communicate directly, respectfully, and with evidence. Critique the change, not the person.

## Expected Behavior

- Share technical reasoning clearly
- Respect review boundaries and maintainer decisions
- Report bugs and risks without hostility
- Prefer reproducible evidence over speculation

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Deliberate misrepresentation of results or evidence
- Attempts to bypass governance or approval boundaries through social pressure

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close unproductive threads, or reject contributions that do not meet these standards.
